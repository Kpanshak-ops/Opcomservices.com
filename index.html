Regulatory Services

From license applications to full regulatory compliance — we handle the complexity so you can focus on your business.

NCC License Applications & Renewals

End-to-end preparation and submission of license applications for new entrants and renewal of existing licenses including ISP, infrastructure, and terminal equipment approvals.

⚖️

Regulatory Compliance Management

Full compliance submissions including Annual Operational Levy (AOL), AOLR assessments, Consumer Code of Practice, and Annual Industry Information Reporting to the NCC.

📡

Infrastructure & Collocation Licensing

Specialist advisory for tower companies and infrastructure providers on collocation service licenses, renewals, and ongoing NCC regulatory obligations.

💻

Value Added Services (VAS) Licensing

Preparation of VAS provider license applications, short code applications, tariff approvals, and equipment type approval documentation for the NCC.

🔌

Terminal Equipment Approval

Advisory and documentation services for companies seeking NCC approval for the sale, installation, and operation of terminal equipment in the Nigerian market.

🏛️

Telecoms & ICT Advisory

Strategic consulting for telecoms operators, ISPs, and technology companies on navigating Nigeria's regulatory environment, licensing strategy, and policy compliance.

About

Victor Christopher Pyarrap

MPhil · MBA · CITM — Founder & Principal Consultant

Victor Christopher Pyarrap brings unparalleled depth to telecoms regulatory consulting in Nigeria. A former Assistant Director at the Nigerian Communications Commission's Executive Commissioner Technical Services (ECTS) division, he spent decades at the centre of Nigeria's telecoms regulatory framework before founding Opcom Services Limited.

  • Former Assistant Director, Executive Commissioner Technical Services (ECTS) — Nigerian Communications Commission (NCC)
  • Over 28 years of professional experience in the Nigerian telecommunications industry
  • Postgraduate credentials: Master of Philosophy (MPhil), Master of Business Administration (MBA), Chartered IT Manager (CITM)
  • Trusted advisor to some of Nigeria's leading telecoms infrastructure and service providers
Current Flagship Project: Serving as lead consultant on an EU-supported Federal Ministry of Communications and Digital Economy initiative — a national project covering 90,000km fiber optic cable deployment and a baseline digital literacy study, targeting 70% broadband penetration and 70% digital literacy in Nigeria by 2027.
